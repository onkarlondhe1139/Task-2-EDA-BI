# Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Intelligence Dashboard — Myntra Sales Dataset

# Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and building a **Business Intelligence dashboard** to uncover insights from a retail sales dataset. The objective was to analyze trends, identify patterns, and extract actionable business intelligence using SQL and visualization tools.

---

# Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across time, geography, and product categories
* Identify top-performing products, sellers, and locations
* Understand customer behavior using ratings and purchase trends
* Design a KPI dashboard for business decision-making

# Visual Insights Breakdown

# Monthly Sales Trend

* Sales declined from January to May and slightly increased in June.
* Indicates possible seasonal demand or marketing impact.

# Daily Sales Trend

* Highest sales recorded on **Sunday and Thursday**
* Lowest sales observed on **Tuesday**
* Suggests strong weekend buying behavior.

# Sales by Gender

* Distribution is fairly balanced.
* Boys category contributes the highest percentage (~27%).

# Sales by State

* Certain states generate significantly higher revenue.
* Indicates region-wise demand concentration.

# Top Cities by Sales

Top performing cities:

1. Hyderabad — ₹39.41M
2. New Delhi — ₹36.25M
3. Chennai — ₹22.91M
4. Ahmedabad — ₹17.60M
5. Surat — ₹17.48M

These cities are major revenue drivers.
